FAQs

Who are we?

It started with 4 lads from Glasgow with a passion for fitness. We wanted to create a specialised platform for like minded people to exchange tips and routines, and here we are!


Membership

We are one of the fastest growing social media platforms of this decade. "A platform like no other" - Donald J Trump.


Does it cost to sign up?

Signing up to fitness fanatic is completely free, simply sign up using the Register link at the top right hand side of the webpage.


How does it work?

Once you've registered, you can view and post workouts and tips to fellow fitness fanatics! You can also upvote/downvote other workouts and leave comments to improve the workout. Think of it as the social media for gym goers.


Who funds us?

We are partially funded by the Russian Government. Don't worry your data is safe.
